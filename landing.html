Child's Play Redesign

Website

childsplaycharity.org

The Purpose

Goal of the project was to redesign a non profit website. We choose redesign child's play charity as we believed there was a lot of potential and we could help increase donations. This project was done as a class project. I worked together with in a team of three. I further iterated on all aspects of this project to make it my own.

My Deliverables

User Interviews
Storyboard
Low Fidelity Desktop Wireframes
High Fidelity Desktop Wireframes
prototypes

The Process

In this project we gathered user insight with a goal of understanding how people interact with charities and fully understand potential pain points. We evaluated competitors to have an understanding of potential opportunities for improvement. We took our findings and implemented a more clean and concise webpage. We presented this to potential users and continued to iterate.

The Problem

We believe that by increasing transparency of donated funds and making options for donations more accessible on the website we will directly impact the sick kids and their families by increasing resources to have fun and reduce trauma while living a tough situation.

User Research and Findings

In this research project, I planned and conducted 3 interviews with potential users Followed that with affinity mapping to analyze results and pinpoint patterns We determined that 100% of people are interested in donating to charity. They are more willing to donate when organization is transparent in what they do.
